# BioSecure AI Minimal Design System

## Brand & Style
A professional, attractive, and minimalist dark mode user interface designed for administrative clarity, speed, and visual appeal. It uses soft glassmorphic containers, subtle borders, and vivid neon-indigo and emerald accents to establish a premium aesthetic.

## Colors
- **Background**: `#09090b` (Zinc-950)
- **Surface**: `rgba(24, 24, 27, 0.65)` (Zinc-900 with transparency)
- **Border**: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.08)`
- **Primary (Accent)**: `#6366f1` (Indigo-500)
- **Success (Present)**: `#10b981` (Emerald-500)
- **Danger (Logout/Delete)**: `#ef4444` (Red-500)
- **Text Primary**: `#fafafa` (Zinc-50)
- **Text Secondary**: `#a1a1aa` (Zinc-400)

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Inter` (geometric, legible)
- **Body**: `Inter`

## Shapes
- **Cards/Containers**: `rounded-xl` (12px radius)
- **Buttons/Inputs**: `rounded-lg` (8px radius)
- **Pills/Tags**: `rounded-full`

## Layering & Blur
- **Backdrop Blur**: `12px`
- **Inner Stroke**: 1px border at 8% white opacity.
